How It Works

Two-Tier Affiliate Program
  • Level 1 (Direct): Earn 10% commission on all purchases made by users you refer directly.
  • Level 2: Earn 5% commission on purchases made by users referred by your Level 1 affiliates.
Commission Process
  1. Commissions are held for 30 days to account for potential refunds or disputes.
  2. After the holding period, commissions are marked as approved and eligible for payout.
  3. Payouts are processed by administrators through PayPal.
  4. You'll receive an email notification when your payout is processed.
