2. Affiliate Status

Upon approval, you will be granted "Affiliate" status, allowing you to earn commissions by referring new customers to GloriaMundo Chat.

3. Commission Structure

Commissions are calculated as follows:

  • Level 1 Commission: 10% of the purchase amount from customers you directly refer.
  • Level 2 Commission: 5% of the purchase amount from customers referred by your direct referrals.
  • Commissions are held for 30 days to allow for potential refunds or chargebacks.
  • Commissions are paid through PayPal to the email address you provide.

4. Referral Attribution

Referrals are attributed as follows:

  • Referral tracking is based on a unique referral code assigned to each affiliate.
  • A referral cookie is stored for 30 days when a user clicks your referral link.
  • If a user completes a purchase within this 30-day window, you will receive commission.
