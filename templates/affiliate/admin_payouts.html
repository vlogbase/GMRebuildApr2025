About PayPal Payouts

  • PayPal Payouts are processed in batches to multiple recipients simultaneously
  • The status of a batch can be checked by clicking the "Check Status" button
  • It may take up to 24 hours for PayPal to fully process payouts
  • Failed payouts will be marked accordingly and can be retried from the Pending Commissions page
  • For issues with PayPal payouts, check your PayPal account credentials and API access
